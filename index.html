Overview

Controls: Player movement is controlled by the Space bar for jumping. The character will jump a distance depending how long the player pressed the spacebar.

Basic Feature: The player needs to control the character to land on the platform to gain points. If the player did not land on the platform, the game will be over and the current score will be uploaded onto the scoreboard.

Advanced Feature: Combo Scoring for Perfect Jumps. If a player successfully lands on the center of the next platform for at least two times, it will create a combo. The player will gain the same amount of points as the combo increase. For example, 2x combo would be 2 points, 5x combo would be 5 points. If the player fails to land on the center of the next platform, the combo will break and the player will gain the default value, which is 1.

How to play

Hold space key, control the jump distance, land on the platform, get points
